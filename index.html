Company Background:

ZFV Enterprises is a business that has been dedicated to providing top-notch hardware materials for the construction industry since its inception on November 24, 2016. It was established at 113 Ramon Magsaysay Street. Tilapayong, Baliuag, Bulacan. The company was founded by Alexander De Dios Villafuerte, a visionary entrepreneur who identified the need for reliable and high-quality construction materials in the industry.

Starting with a focus on hardware materials for construction, ZFV Enterprises quickly expanded its offerings to include rental services for scaffolding, installation services for CCTV systems, and delivery services for wholesale orders of construction materials like roofing, plumbing, electricals and more. Thereupon, with a commitment to excellence in every aspect of the business, ZFV Enterprises has rapidly established itself as a pioneer in the industry.

Over the years, the company has built a reputation for exceptional customer service, timely delivery, and incomparable quality. ZFV Enterprises is proud to serve a diverse range of customers, including contractors, builders, and homeowners, providing them with the tools and resources they need to complete their projects on time and within budget. With a steadfast commitment to innovation and a customer-first approach, ZFV Enterprises continues to be a trusted partner for all construction needs.
